Choose the time period this report should cover. The report will only include withdrawal records from this period, but the inventory snapshot always shows current levels.

What's included in each report
📦
1. Inventory Snapshot

Every item, current stock level, category, location and status at the time the report was generated.

📋
2. Withdrawal History

All items taken from the store within the selected period — who took it, when, how much, and which batch it came from.

📅
3. Expiry Report

All batches with expiry dates — expired, critical (within 7 days), and upcoming (within 30 days) — with exact days remaining.

💰
4. Restock Estimate

Items that need restocking with estimated costs based on the last recorded purchase price per item. Grand total included.

ℹ️ Excel puts each section on a separate colour-coded sheet — ideal for sharing or further analysis.
PDF is a formatted document — ideal for printing and filing.
